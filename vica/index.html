VICA

VICA is a restrained, safety-first race-awareness layer for VR sim racing. It is designed to surface only the information needed to maintain focus.

What to expect

  • Minimal visual load for low cognitive overhead.
  • Clear race-critical cues in predictable positions.
  • Conservative defaults and explicit stop paths.
  • Static public page while intake and releases remain alpha-stage.

Safety stance

  • VICA is driver support tooling, never a substitute for judgment.
  • Use in controlled sessions first before any competitive use.
  • Disable quickly if visibility or focus is impaired.
